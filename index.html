Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our modded Android devices, NetHunter setups, and additional services.

What is included with a modded device from ViP3R Hunter Devices?

Our modded devices come pre-installed with Kali NetHunter, a NetHunter-supported kernel, and a custom ROM. We also perform rigorous quality checks to ensure optimal performance. Additionally, we try to include the original stock charger and cable whenever possible, but since these are pre-owned devices, we cannot guarantee their availability.

Can I choose a custom ROM for my device?

Yes, you can select a custom ROM of your choice, or you can opt for one of our recommended ROMs to ensure seamless performance and security.

Do you provide support after purchasing a modded device?

Yes, we offer technical support if you encounter any issues. Our team is available to assist with troubleshooting and inquiries. However, we do not provide support for future OS updates. If you receive a new custom ROM in the future, you can purchase a kernel patch add-on to maintain NetHunter support and ensure continued optimal performance and security.

How do I order a fully modded Android device?

Simply visit our website, choose your preferred device, and specify your customization preferences. We’ll handle the rest and notify you once your device is ready to be shipped.

Do you offer cybersecurity add-ons or custom skins?

Yes! We plan to introduce cybersecurity-themed device skins, back covers, and other add-ons soon to enhance the look and feel of your modded device.

How secure are ViP3R Hunter Devices?

Our devices are configured with top-notch security measures, including NetHunter kernel support and custom ROMs optimized for security, ensuring a secure and reliable environment for penetration testing.
